About Us

We are Bradley, Sean, Vitalina, and Eoghan, a group of third-year students studying Information Technology and Cybersecurity at MTU. This project, supervised by Hristo Trifonov, explores how cryptography and encryption contribute to modern cybersecurity practices.

Our goal is to raise awareness of the importance of data protection and secure communications. Through this project, we demonstrate how encryption can help protect organisations and individuals from cyber threats.

Planned Clientele – Irish Companies

  • Banks: Bank of Ireland, AIB, PTSB
  • Universities: MTU
