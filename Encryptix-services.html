Symmetric encryption - Sean

One shared key for both encrypt and decrypt. Useful for encrypting local export folders or quick notes.

Asymmetric encryption - Eoghan

Public key to encrypt and private key to decrypt. Useful when emailing another clinic since you do not share secrets first.

Hashing - Bradley

One way fingerprint of data. Good for checking a lab file did not change during transfer.

Hybrid encryption - Vitalina

Combine both styles. Encrypt the message with a fast symmetric key, then protect that key with a public key.
